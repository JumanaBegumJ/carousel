MAHENDRA SINGH DHONI
Right handed Batsman and Wicket keeper

Mahendra Singh Dhoni born on 7 July 1981 is an Indian professional cricketer who plays as a right handed batter and a wicket-keeper. Dhoni is also known for his cool on the field which has earned him the monicker "Captain cool".

RAVINDRA JADEJA
All Rounder (Left Handed)

Ravindra Jadeja (born 6 December 1988)is an Indian international cricketer represents the Indian national cricket team. In the final of 2023 Indian Premier League final, Chennai Super Kings required 10 from the final two balls. Jadeja hit a 6 and 4 to give CSK the title.

MATHEESHA PATHIRANA
Right Arm Bowler

Matheesha Pathirana (born 18 December 2002) is a professional Sri Lankan cricketer who currently plays limited overs cricket for the national team. He has been referred to as Baby Malinga, as he has modelled his bowling action
